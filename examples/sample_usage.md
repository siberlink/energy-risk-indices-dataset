# Sample Usage

This dataset can be used for:

- Energy risk dashboards
- Time-series analysis
- Market commentary
- Geopolitical risk research
- Trading and macro research

## Example Questions

- Is global energy risk rising or falling?
- Is European risk diverging from global risk?
- Is gas system stress increasing before price volatility?
- Do geopolitical alerts correlate with Brent, TTF, or VIX movements?

## Suggested Visualizations

- Line chart of GERI, EERI, and EGSI over time
- 7-day moving average
- Risk band heatmap
- Index comparison table
- Correlation chart with Brent, TTF, VIX, or EUR/USD
